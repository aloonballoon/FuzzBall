• 1. Click to draw walls. Right click to switch between vertical and horizontal walls
  • 2. If a wall is built with no fuzzballs on a side, that area will fill in and is "captured". Captured area adds to your score
  • 3. If a fuzzball hits a wall as it is being built the wall disappears and you will lose a life
  • 4. Once both sides of a growing wall hit either another wall or the game edge, the wall solidifies and the balls can no longer pass through
  • 5. Claim the target percentage of area to advance levels
